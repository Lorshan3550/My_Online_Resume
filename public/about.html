Lorshan's Web Development Journey

Welcome to Lorshan's web development portfolio! Here you will find a showcase of my

My Journey into Web Development

Ever since I first tinkered with HTML and CSS, I knew that web development was my true calling.
I embarked on a journey to expand my knowledge and skills, constantly pushing the boundaries
of what I could achieve. Through extensive self-study, online courses, and hands-on projects, I
honed my abilities in various programming languages, frameworks, and content management systems.

Areas of Expertise

Front-end Development

I specialize in crafting visually appealing and intuitive user interfaces

Back-end Development

I am proficient in server-side programming languages like PHP

Content Management Systems (CMS)

I have hands-on experience working with popular CMS
platforms such as WordPress and Drupal. Whether it's customizing themes, creating custom

E-commerce Solutions

I have worked on numerous e-commerce projects, utilizing platforms
